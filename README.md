## Project Overview
Created an interactive e-commerce sales dashboard analyzing 10K+ transactions across 4 regions and 17 product categories.

## Dashboard Components

### 1. Revenue Trend (Line Chart)
- Daily sales with 7-day moving average
- Regional breakdown via color
- **Insight:** Revenue peaks in Q4, with West region driving 35% of total sales

### 2. Category Performance (Horizontal Bar)
- Sales and profit by category
- Identifies which categories are profitable vs. loss-making
- **Insight:** Technology is high revenue but low margin; Office Supplies is profitable

### 3. Regional Heatmap (Heatmap Chart)
- Sales intensity by region and sub-category
- Hot spots show high-performing combos
- **Insight:** West + Technology and South + Furniture are top performers

### 4. Key Metrics (KPI Cards)
- Total Revenue: $[amount]
- Average Order Value: $[amount]
- Unique Customers: [number]
- Profit Margin: [%]

### 5. Customer Segments (Bar Chart)
- LTV (Lifetime Value) by segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
- **Insight:** Corporate customers have 3x higher LTV than consumers
